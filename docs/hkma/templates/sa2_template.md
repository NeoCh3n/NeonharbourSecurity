# HKMA SA-2 Control Template

## Control Objective
Document the security management framework with clear ownership, review cadence, and escalation.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- Board / Audit Committee
- Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
- Technology Risk (1st line)
- Internal Audit (3rd line)

## Evidence Checklist
- Approved Information Security Policy (English + Traditional Chinese)
- Annual review meeting minutes
- Org chart with named owners
- Exceptions log and remediation tracker
