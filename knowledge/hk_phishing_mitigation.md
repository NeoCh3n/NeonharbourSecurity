# Hong Kong Phishing Mitigation Guidance

- Monitor HKCERT advisories for language-specific lures.
- Enforce DMARC reject policy for banking domains.
- Deploy SMS filtering for HK local carriers (CSL, HKT, SmarTone).
- Provide bilingual security awareness with HKMA e-banking best practices.
- Document rapid takedown procedure with HK police and domain registrars.
