# Third-Party Risk in HK Financial Services

- Maintain register of material outsourcing arrangements under HKMA SPM OR-2.
- Require SOC 2 Type II or ISO/IEC 27001 for cloud-native vendors.
- Quarterly assurance questionnaire covering data residency, breach history, subcontractors.
- Trigger enhanced monitoring when vendor access originates outside Hong Kong / Singapore / Japan.
