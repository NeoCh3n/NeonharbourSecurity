# HKMA TM-G-1 Key Requirements

## Technology Risk Management Themes
1. Governance: board accountability, clear risk appetite, oversight committees.
2. Risk identification: annual assessments of emerging threats to HK retail banking.
3. Preventive controls: network segmentation, privileged identity management, secure SDLC.
4. Detection & response: 24/7 monitoring, MTTA/MTTR tracking, playbook rehearsals.
5. Information sharing: HKMA collaboration centres, FS-ISAC membership.

## Compliance Evidence Suggestions
- Risk committee agendas & decisions referencing TM-G-1 clauses.
- SOC runbooks for ransomware, payments fraud, and e-banking outages.
- Metrics pack showing variance analysis vs tolerance thresholds.
